# AidSync — Brand Voice

## Core Brand Position

AidSync is not a flashy consumer health app.
It is a calm, credible field tool built for clinicians working under pressure in unstable environments.

The brand should feel:
- reliable
- practical
- low-drama
- clinically respectful
- quietly capable

It should never feel:
- hypey
- futuristic-for-the-sake-of-it
- military fetishist
- startup-bro clever
- pseudo-medical

---

## Brand Promise

**Capture care locally. Check medication safety. Sync when the network returns.**

---

## Voice Attributes

### 1. Calm
Short, direct language.
No panic language.
No melodrama.

### 2. Practical
Focus on what the app does, not abstract transformation jargon.

### 3. Safety-aware
Use careful wording around medication support.
Never imply diagnosis or autonomous prescribing.

### 4. Human
The app supports clinicians and patients in difficult environments.
It is serious without sounding cold.

### 5. Quietly technical
Do not oversell the AI.
Do not oversell the sync engine.
Present both as dependable systems doing useful work.

---

## Messaging Pillars

### Offline-first care continuity
Care workflows should continue even when connectivity fails.

### Medication safety support
Paper inserts become structured safety checks tied to patient context.

### Trust through transparency
Warnings must explain why they were triggered.

### Sync as infrastructure, not ceremony
The product keeps working and syncs when possible.

### Voice capture under field conditions
Clinicians can capture notes quickly without breaking flow.

---

## Tone Examples

### Good
- Works offline and syncs later.
- Scan a medicine insert and check it against patient history.
- Review warnings before saving the encounter.
- Keep field records available when the network is unreliable.
- Capture notes while treating the patient.

### Bad
- AI-powered medical superintelligence for the edge battlefield.
- Revolutionizing care with autonomous diagnosis.
- Never make a medication mistake again.
- Military-grade emergency prescribing copilot.

## Tagline Options

- **Field care, even offline.**
- **Scan. Check. Record. Sync.**
- **Medication safety for disconnected environments.**
- **Local-first care records for the field.**

Recommended:
**Scan. Check. Record. Sync.**

---

## Elevator Pitch

AidSync is an offline-first field care app that helps clinicians capture patient encounters, record voice notes, scan medicine inserts, run medication safety checks against patient context, and sync records when connectivity returns.

---

## One-line Product Descriptions

### Short
AidSync helps field clinicians record encounters and run medication safety checks offline.

### Medium
AidSync is a local-first field care app that turns scanned medicine inserts into structured safety checks linked to patient history.

### Hackathon submission style
AidSync is an offline-first clinical workflow app for low-connectivity environments. It lets clinicians scan medicine inserts, structure the extracted medication data, compare it against patient allergies, conditions, and current medications, and save the safety check into the encounter history before syncing through PowerSync.

---

## Writing Rules

- Prefer plain English over grandiose language
- Prefer “supports” over “replaces”
- Prefer “warning” over “decision”
- Prefer “clinician review” over “automatic action”
- Avoid certainty language unless deterministic and true

---

## Forbidden Phrases

- autonomous diagnosis
- battlefield dominance
- combat medicine AI
- perfect prescribing
- guaranteed safe treatment
- replaces clinician judgment
- military-grade
